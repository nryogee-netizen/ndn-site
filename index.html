Introduction

NDN Controls and Automation was started in Bengaluru, Karnataka. The company was formed by a professional automation team with extensive experience in the design and integration of industrial automation systems including PLC, HMI, SCADA, VFDs, servo drives, soft starters and electrical control panels.

We provide weighing automation, batching systems, tank weighing and other industrial weighing solutions. Our multidisciplinary engineering and management expertise enables us to deliver end-to-end solutions from concept through commissioning.
