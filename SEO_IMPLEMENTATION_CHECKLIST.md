## ❓ FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: When will search engines show the full company name?**
A: It typically takes 2-4 weeks for Google to recrawl and re-index your pages. You'll see the new title and description in Google Search Console immediately after re-crawling.

**Q: Do I need to do anything special after making changes?**
A: Yes! After updating your HTML files:
1. Go to Google Search Console
2. Request indexing for your main page
3. Monitor the Performance tab for keyword rankings

**Q: How often should I check my rankings?**
A: Check weekly initially, then monthly once stable. Use Google Search Console for free tracking.

**Q: Can I edit these changes later?**
A: Yes! You can always update meta tags, headings, and content. Just remember to request re-indexing in GSC.

**Q: What if nothing changes after a month?**
A: This is normal. SEO takes time. Ensure:
- [ ] Pages are indexed (check GSC)
- [ ] No crawl errors
- [ ] Mobile-friendly status is PASS
- [ ] Schema is valid (check validator.schema.org)
